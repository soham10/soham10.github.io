Projects

Pinpricks on Vortex: Star Cluster Analysis

Worked on a Computational Astronomy project focused on star cluster analysis, as part of the Krittika Summer Projects organized by Krittika, the Astronomy Club, IIT Bombay. Leveraged data from the Hubble Space Telescope and the PHANGS survey to analyze the distribution of stars and gas, as well as determine the ages of star clusters. The project employed aperture photometry and color-color diagrams to extract and interpret astrophysical properties. Link to my project report: Project Report.

Elementary Particle Physics

Participated in a three-month reading project under the Summer of Science initiative by the Maths and Physics Club, IIT Bombay. The project focused on Elementary Particle Physics, where I built a strong foundation by starting with classical and quantum mechanics and progressively explored advanced topics such as symmetries and quantum field theory. The approach was structured to develop an in-depth understanding, layer by layer, of this fascinating domain. Link to my project report: Summer Report.

Turbulence: Non Linear Dynamics Course Project

As part of my Nonlinear Dynamics project, I delved into the study of the Navier-Stokes (NS) equations and the complex nature of turbulence, examining their real-world applications and implications. I simulated fluid flow around a cylindrical obstacle, varying the Reynolds number, using the Lattice Boltzmann method in Python to visualize how turbulence evolves under different conditions. Additionally, I constructed a bifurcation diagram to analyze and better understand the transition to turbulence. This work sparked engaging discussions on why turbulence is not only a subject of ongoing scientific debate but also an intriguing and captivating area of study. Link to my project report: Presentation.

NetWeavers: Neural Networks from Scratch

I participated in a Winter in Data Science project hosted by the Analytics Club at IIT Bombay, where I developed a neural network to identify and predict numeric digits using the MNIST dataset. Link to my project report: Winter Report.
