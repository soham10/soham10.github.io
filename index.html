About Me

Hey there! I'm Soham, a so-called nerd (or whatever they say in Gen Z lingo), juggling my love for physics, math, rockets, machine learning, cars, cricket, and you. Whether it's solving puzzles, speed-cubing with my Rubik’s cube, or diving into cool projects that turn nerdy ideas into real-world magic, I’m always up for a challenge.
